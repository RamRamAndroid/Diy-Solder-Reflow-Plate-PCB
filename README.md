# Diy-Solder-Reflow-Plate-PCB
A Diy Solder Reflow Plate Made Of 4 Components And A PCB

# Details
70mm x 50mm 

12VDC (5A Minimum)

5.5mm x 2.5mm Barrel Jack

# Why Did I Make This
Solder reflow plates online are too expencive and small so i made a cheaper better version

# How To Build
First download the gerber file in the releases and then get it manufactured at pcb way or jlc pcb and order a 100uf 50v aluminum electrolytic surface mount capacitor and a 12v 5a power supply and a dc barrel jack and a ksd9700 then solder the cap in its place beside the dc jack and solder the dc jack in its place and then solder the ksd9700 to the pads labelled ksd9700 and wait for it to cool down and mount the ksd9700 behind the hot plate surface with thermal pads or thermal paste and turn it on and wait for 30sec and u can solder with it
